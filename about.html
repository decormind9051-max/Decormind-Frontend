About Decormind

A detail-obsessed interior studio crafting warm, practical homes across Kolkata and beyond.

Studio director

The mind behind Decormind

Director Name

Founder & Creative Director, Decormind

“How should this space feel when you walk in at the end of a long day?” is the starting point for every Decormind home. Everything else — layouts, materials, storage, lighting — is built around that feeling.

Led by a director who has spent years on real sites across Kolkata and beyond, the studio understands both the poetry of design and the discipline of execution.

That means one clear vision from concept to handover, fewer surprises on site, and a home that quietly reflects the way you and your family actually live.

What we offer

Interior design support that fits your life

NRI & out-of-city projects

We design and execute homes for NRIs and out-of-city owners who can’t be on site every day. From detailed video walk-throughs to clear milestone updates, you stay in control without having to manage vendors.

Layouts, material selections, and site decisions are shared with you in an organised way so handover feels smooth, not stressful.

Design videos & walkthroughs

Before anything is built, we share clear 3D views and video walkthroughs so you can experience the space, tweak details, and sign off with confidence.

This makes it easy for your family to visualise finishes, furniture, and lighting — and ensures the final home matches what you saw on screen.
