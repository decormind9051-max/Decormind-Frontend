Privacy Policy

How Decormind collects, uses, and protects your information throughout your inquiry and project.

Last updated: December 2025

How we handle your data

Decormind is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you browse our site, request a quotation, or work with our team.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details such as your name, phone number, email address, and city.
  • Project information such as property type, location, budget range, and design preferences.
  • Website usage data such as pages visited and actions taken, collected via basic analytics tools.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule design consultations.
  • To prepare quotations, proposals, and project documentation.
  • To improve our website, services, and communication based on anonymized usage data.

3. Sharing and third parties

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party.
  • We may share limited details with trusted service providers (for example, technology partners or contractors) strictly to deliver agreed services.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data retention and security

  • We retain inquiry and project data only for as long as reasonably necessary for our work together and for legal or accounting purposes.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Your choices

  • You can ask us to update or correct your contact details at any time.
  • You may request that we delete your information where it is no longer required for ongoing work or legal purposes.
  • You can opt out of non-essential marketing communication by contacting us.

6. Contact for privacy questions
